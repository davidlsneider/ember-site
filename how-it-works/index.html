How it works

Your product, inside a boundary your customer controls.

Sovereign Mode separates your software from readable custody of your customer’s data—without turning the customer into your infrastructure team.

01 · Configure

You define the product. Package the application and the models, tools, storage, and connections it needs.

02 · Approve

Your customer defines the boundary. They approve the exact workload before sensitive data enters it.

03 · Run

Ember operates the sealed environment. The customer keeps using your product; its data becomes readable only inside the approved workload.

04 · Verify

The boundary cannot widen silently. New code or capabilities require new approval, and each session leaves verifiable evidence of what ran.

The customer gets the control it wants from BYOC. You keep the product experience. Neither side takes on another private deployment.
