EMBER · THE THREE DIAGRAMS

D1 — Custody, before and after

AUDIENCE: THE VENDOR CEO · OWNS: THE INVERSION

The vendor serves the entire product and can read none of it.

D2 — One session, precisely

AUDIENCE: THE VENDOR'S ENGINEERS · OWNS: THE RUNTIME PATH

The whole session happens inside the vendor's product. The content is readable in exactly two places — the app on the user's device, and the attested room. The vendor gets metadata back, never plaintext.

D3 — The upgrade

AUDIENCE: THE END CUSTOMER AND THEIR AUDITOR · OWNS: THE MOST SENSITIVE OPERATION

No code reaches the room without on-chain M-of-N approval. Upgrading is the most sensitive operation in the system — the room only boots builds the multisig has approved, and the customer can check the attestation against the chain.
